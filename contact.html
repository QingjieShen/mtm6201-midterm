FAQ

What is Dream Night?

Dream Night is an aroma oil brand that supports DIY formulas. We offer a wide range of high-quality aroma oils that can be used in a variety of applications, such as diffusers, candles, and beauty products.

How do I use Dream Night aroma oils?

Our aroma oils can be used in a variety of ways, depending on the application. For example, you can add a few drops of oil to a diffuser to fill your space with a pleasant scent, or mix the oils with carrier oils to create your own beauty and wellness products.

Are Dream Night products safe?

Yes, our aroma oils are made with high-quality ingredients and are safe for use in a variety of applications. However, it's important to follow the instructions on the product packaging and use the oils in a well-ventilated area.

Where can I buy Dream Night products?

Our products are available for purchase online through our website, or at our physical store in Ottawa, Canada.

Can I use Dream Night oils for aromatherapy?

Yes, our oils can be used for aromatherapy purposes. However, we recommend consulting with a qualified aromatherapist before using our oils for therapeutic purposes.
